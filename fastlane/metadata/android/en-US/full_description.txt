Brick Blast is a fast, offline brick-breaker built with Jetpack Compose.

Drag to aim, release to launch a stream of balls, and bounce them off the
walls to smash the descending bricks before they reach the bottom. Each round
the bricks move down and a new row appears, so plan your angles carefully.

Features:

* Turn-based aim-and-shoot gameplay with a trajectory preview
* Custom physics with realistic bouncing and a smooth 60 FPS game loop
* Collectable extra balls that grow your arsenal every round
* Power-ups: bomb, laser, multi-ball and double damage
* Multiple modes: Classic, Challenge, Time Attack and Hardcore
* Particle effects and satisfying brick-destruction animations
* Light and dark themes with an animated theme switch
* High-score tracking and statistics
* Share your score as an image
* Completely offline - no ads, no tracking, no internet permission

Break. Bounce. Blast.
